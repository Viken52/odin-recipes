Beef Stew

Description

This traditional Beef Stew is the best Slow Cooker recipe for Fall and Winter. Itâ€™s loaded with tender bites of juicy beef, golden potatoes, carrots, peas, onions, garlic, celery, and a hearty beef broth.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs Beed Cubes
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 1 1/2 cup Beef Broth
  • 1 tsp wocestershire sauce
  • 1 Clove Garlic
  • 1 Bay Leaf
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 4 Carrots peeled/sliced
  • 3 Potatoes Cubed
  • 1 Onion sliced
  • 1 Stalk Celery sliced

Instructions

  1. Put meat in crock-pot
  2. Mix flour, salt & pepper
  3. Pour over mean and stir to coat meat
  4. Add remaining ingredients & stir to mix well
  5. Cover crock-pot on low for 10-12 hours or on high 4-6 hours
